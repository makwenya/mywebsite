About Unity High School

Inspiring Excellence, Unity & Leadership

Our History

Founded in Maseru, Lesotho, Unity High School was established to meet the growing demand for quality English-medium education in the region. Since its inception, the school has strived to create a nurturing environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. With a focus on unity, integrity, and leadership, Unity High School continues to shape future leaders who are confident, informed, and globally minded.

Leadership Team

Principal: Mrs. Mpho Mofokeng – A passionate educator with over 20 years of experience in school leadership and curriculum development.

Deputy Principal: Mr. Thabo Letsie – Oversees student discipline and academic performance with a focus on continuous improvement.

Academic Coordinator: Ms. Naleli Makhetha – Leads curriculum planning and assessment across all departments.

Our Core Values

  • Unity: Promoting harmony, collaboration, and respect among students and staff.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in teaching, learning, and behavior.
  • Integrity: Encouraging honesty, responsibility, and ethical behavior in all aspects of school life.
  • Innovation: Fostering creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.
  • Leadership: Empowering students to be proactive, responsible, and compassionate leaders.
